Become a Channel Partner

We offer sales partnerships to competent companies having a presence in the education / ERP domain and want to expand their portfolio by including SKOOLZOOM. The primary role of the partner company would be to offer and promote SKOOLZOOM ERP in their mutually assigned geographies, be the face of Mozzine Technologies, and drive sales. We provide complete training and orientation to help partners gain knowledge and come to pace.

Home-Partnership
WHAT ARE THE BENEFITS?

Benefits for Channel Partner.

  • Attractive Partner Margin programme
  • Suitable prospect to be a part of a positively growing education sector
  • Quick and efficient pre-sales and post-sales support
  • Trusted partnership & opportunity for shared growth with Mozzine
  • Preference in upcoming future opportunities with Mozzine
  • Email Support
  • Training and certification for SKOOLZOOM
Responsibilities of Channel Partner

Role and Responsibilities.

  • Developing local market
  • Performing presales activities (e.g., client prospecting, reference sharing)
  • Qualifying leads (Suspects and Prospects)
  • Driving leads to closure with assistance from Mozzine
  • Flourishing the SKOOLZOOM brand
  • Assisting Mozzine in sales (like price negotiation and deal closure)
  • Performing activities like scope management and account management of the identified customers
  • Driving implementation activities like Gap Analysis, product configuration and user training along with post-implementation support
